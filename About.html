About us

Placeholder.com is the world's largest freelancing and crowd sourcing marketplace by number of users and projects. We connect a lot of employers and freelancers globally from many countries, regions and territories. Through our marketplace, employers can hire freelancers to do work in areas such as software development, writing, data entry and design right through to engineering, the sciences, sales and marketing, accounting and legal services.
